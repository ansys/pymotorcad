show_results_viewer_lab#

MotorCAD.show_results_viewer_lab(calculation_type)#

Load the results viewer for the specified Lab calculation type.

Parameters:
calculation_typestr

Type of calculation. Options are "Electromagnetic", "Thermal", "Generator", "Duty Cycle", and "Calibration".
